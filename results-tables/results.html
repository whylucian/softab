Key Findings

  • Vulkan dominates token generation (tg) across nearly all model sizes
  • ROCm hipblaslt-0 wins prompt processing (pp) for most dense models and smaller MoE
  • MoE models strongly favor Vulkan - up to 2.7x faster pp on 120B MoE (488 vs 180 t/s)
  • Dense 70B+ models favor ROCm for pp but Vulkan still wins tg
  • vulkan-radv-ub1024 is consistently top performer for Vulkan
  • Q8 quantization hurts ROCm performance - llama3.2-3b-q8 is 2x slower on ROCm vs Vulkan
